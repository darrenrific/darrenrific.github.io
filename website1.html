Hi there! My name, as you may have guessed, is Darren Vogt.

I am an artist from Louisville, Kentucky, with special expertise in the fields of graphic design, animation, and illustration. More than anything I'm interested in designs and artworks that evoke a sense of fun and escape. Good art should change the world around us, and in my case, I'm making it brighter.

I've worked as a cartoonist online and in print for five years and a graphic designer for two, and recently I've become interested in visual effects, animation, and motion graphics.
